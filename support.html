Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cine Mix?

Cine Mix is a creativity tool that generates original stories inspired by themes and moods from movies and TV. You can pick 2–4 sources, tweak options like genre, POV, tone, and ending, and get a fresh, transformative story. We do not reproduce copyrighted scripts or characters verbatim.

Does it stream movies or use scripts?

No. Cine Mix does not stream movies or provide full scripts. It uses short, generalized metadata (e.g., titles, tags, loglines) and your chosen options to guide the AI. Output is new writing, not copied text.

Why do I need 2–4 sources?

Blending multiple inspirations helps the AI create more transformative and surprising ideas while avoiding close similarity to any single source.

What about age ratings?

You can set an age rating (e.g., PG‑13) and we’ll guide the output accordingly. Explicit sexual content and graphic violence are disallowed.

Can I save or share my stories?

Yes. Save favorites, export to text/PDF, or regenerate with alternate endings. Your stories remain yours to keep and share.

Is my data used to train AI models?

We do not sell your data. Model providers may process prompts to deliver the service; we disable training on customer content where the provider allows.

Privacy Policy (Detailed)

Effective date: October 1, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your data.

1. Data We Process

  • User content: prompts, options (genre/POV/etc.), generated stories.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs, performance metrics.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (story generation, favorites, history, remix).
  • Customer support and fraud prevention.
  • Analytics to improve UX (aggregated/limited; you can opt out in settings).

3. AI & Third‑Party Services

To generate stories, we send minimal necessary context to AI model providers (e.g., prompt text, high‑level metadata). Where providers allow, we disable using your content to train their models. We may use: cloud hosting, crash analytics, app store billing, and The Movie Database (TMDb) for metadata. This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with processors necessary to operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics) under data protection agreements.

5. International Transfers

Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) as required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above or to comply with legal obligations. You can delete your stories and account at any time from the app or by contacting support.

8. Children

Cine Mix is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain regions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you in‑app or via this page with a new effective date.
